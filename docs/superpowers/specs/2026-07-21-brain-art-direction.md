# Brain World art direction — the north star

Date: 2026-07-21. Chosen by Nathan from AI-generated concept images
(Higgsfield CLI; process in `2026-07-21-higgsfield-brain-inspiration-design.md`).
Reference images live in `docs/superpowers/inspiration/2026-07-21-brain-journey/north-star/`
(git-ignored, local only): `cover.png`, `cover-living.mp4`, `inside.png`, `chamber.png`.

## The look in one sentence

A brain made of starlight in deep space: silver-white particle dust forms real anatomy,
and thinking appears as eight coloured points of light that fire and connect.

## Cover (`cover.png`, animated `cover-living.mp4`)

- Anatomically believable side-profile brain (gyri, cerebellum, stem) formed entirely
  from thousands of tiny silver-white star particles; denser particle bands trace the folds.
- Deep near-black space background with the faintest blue-green nebula hints, sparse specks.
- The 8 move colours appear as small glowing dots/constellations INSIDE the brain.
- Brain sits in the right two-thirds; left third is clear dark space for the headline.
- Motion (Seedance Mini reference): stars twinkle gently, dust drifts very slowly,
  near-static camera with an extremely slow push-in. The brain never morphs or rotates.

## Inside view (`inside.png`)

- NEURONS, not constellations (constellation version explicitly rejected).
- Eight luminous neuron cells with branching dendrites, one per move colour
  (red, purple, gold, blue, green, yellow, orange, violet-grey), at varied depths.
- Long thin axon pathways link them; bright signal pulses travel along the pathways —
  thinking as firing signals.
- Painterly, dense, sparkling (Seedream flavour); silver star-dust fills the space.
- Labels sit in dark pockets near each cluster.

## Chamber (`chamber.png`)

- The full particle brain seen slightly wider; ONE move constellation (its colour only)
  glows at centre inside a soft circular halo ring.
- All other moves are tiny, dim, distant. Calm and reverent — no flashing (rejected earlier).

## Palette anchors

- Space: near-black #0b0b12 family; particles silver-white with cool blue cores.
- The 8 Wesley move colours are the ONLY saturated colour, always as points of light.
- Text on night: light ink #e8e9f2, muted #9fa0b2 (existing bw-on-night values).
